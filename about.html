About Annie Worford

My Story

I’m a UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design, the arts, and writing. I’m passionate about travel and exploring other cultures, and I was recently evacuated from Peace Corps The Gambia for Covid-19. Now, I’m excited about turning my storytelling and design skills to user experience and user interface design.

In my free time, I write novels, create zines, run a sustainable fashion and DIY blog, and go on long(ish) runs.
